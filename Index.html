Projects

SECMC Mining

SECMC has undertaken the execution of mining operations for coal extraction
in the Thar coalfield, Block II, as a means of tackling energy deficiency across
Pakistan. In so doing, SECMC has made several investments which include,
but not limited to, the acquisition of land for the resettlement of communities,
mining facilities and services, and power plants

SECMC Power Plant

Corporate Social Responsibility

In line with international and local policies and guidelines, Sindh Engro Coal Mining Company
as part of its Corporate Social Responsibility activities is under the obligation to provide its
community with facilities for long term development and stability of its people.SECMC will
work in partnership with local communities to build a community governancesystem based
on self-reliance and it will also liase with National and International organizations, NGOs, UN
agencies, private sector and Government authorities to mobilizeresources and facilitate its CSR
activities in the Block. The CSR program is aligned with four focus areas: Education, Health,
Livelihoods and Skill Development.
